Wack-a-LED

Play on Wack-a-Mole where there are 5 LED’s lighted in a random sequence and the hitting the correct photoresistor (which utilizes ADMUX) symbolizes wacking the LED.

Along with start instructions, the top three scores are also displayed which are stored in EEPROM.The random sequence of these LED’s is computed and transferred using USART.
